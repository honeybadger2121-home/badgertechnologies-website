Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Organization details and business information
  • Technical requirements and system details
  • Assessment requests and project specifications

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Website usage analytics (Google Analytics)
  • IP addresses and browser information
  • System performance data during assessments
  • Security logs and monitoring data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide IT services and technical support
  • Conduct security assessments and audits
  • Communicate about services and projects
  • Improve our services and website functionality
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Send relevant service updates and security alerts

3. HIPAA Compliance

For healthcare clients, we maintain HIPAA compliance:

  • All staff sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access controls and audit logs are maintained
  • Regular security training and risk assessments
  • Breach notification procedures are in place

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In connection with business transfers (with notice)

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
  • Multi-factor authentication for all systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with physical access controls
  • Employee background checks and security training
  • Incident response and data breach procedures

6. Data Retention

We retain information for the following periods:

  • Contact information: 3 years after last contact
  • Project documentation: 7 years for compliance
  • Security logs: 2 years minimum
  • Financial records: 7 years per tax requirements
  • Assessment reports: 5 years or as agreed

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability for your information
  • File complaints with regulatory authorities

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies to improve user experience
  • Performance cookies to monitor website speed

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics
  • Netlify: Website hosting and form processing
  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud infrastructure and security
  • Email Services: Business communication

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where we or our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
