Our Story

A new venture built on years of proven IT expertise and a passion for helping businesses succeed.

The Foundation

2019-2025

Built on a solid educational foundation with an Associate's Degree in Applied Science from trade school, my journey into IT began with hands-on experience in telecommunications at major industry leaders. Working at Comcast and AT&T provided invaluable expertise in cabling, network infrastructure, and large-scale system operations.

This foundation evolved into comprehensive IT expertise spanning cybersecurity, network administration, help desk support, and cloud systems management. Years of real-world experience combined with CompTIA certifications and Azure cloud specialization created the technical backbone for Badger Technologies.
