Security Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

Last updated: September 12, 2025

Our Commitment to Security

At Badger Technologies, we take the security of our systems, services, and client data seriously. We appreciate the efforts of security researchers who help us maintain the highest security standards through responsible disclosure of potential vulnerabilities.

Scope

This policy applies to vulnerabilities found in:

  • Our website (badgertechnologies.red-moon-31c4.workers.dev)
  • Client-facing applications and services
  • Infrastructure components we manage
  • Third-party services we directly control

Out of scope: Third-party services not under our direct control, physical security issues, social engineering attacks.

Reporting Guidelines

To report a security vulnerability:

  1. Email us at security@badgertechnologies.us
  2. Include detailed information about the vulnerability
  3. Provide steps to reproduce the issue
  4. Include any proof-of-concept code or screenshots
  5. Use encryption if sensitive data is involved

What We Promise

  • Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  • Provide regular updates on our investigation
  • Work with you to understand and resolve the issue
  • Give credit for the discovery (if desired)
  • Not pursue legal action for good faith security research

What We Ask

  • Give us reasonable time to investigate and fix issues
  • Don't access or modify data that isn't yours
  • Don't disrupt our services or systems
  • Don't publicly disclose the vulnerability until we've addressed it
  • Act in good faith and avoid privacy violations

Response Timeline

  • Critical vulnerabilities: 24-48 hours
  • High-severity issues: 3-5 business days
  • Medium-severity issues: 1-2 weeks
  • Low-severity issues: 2-4 weeks

Recognition

We maintain a security acknowledgments page to recognize researchers who help improve our security posture. If you prefer to remain anonymous, please let us know.
